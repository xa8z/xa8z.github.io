Welcome to Xi'an No.8 Prison!

西安市第八监狱是一所拥有悠久历史和优秀传统的监狱，我们致力于为囚犯提供良好的教育资源和改造环境，帮助他们重新健康、自立、自强。
Xi'an No. 8 Prison is a prison with a long history and excellent traditions, and we are committed to providing good educational resources and a rehabilitating environment for prisoners to help them regain their health, self-reliance and self-reliance.

我们的狱警队伍庞大而优秀，拥有丰富的执法经验和专业知识，我们的管理方法和矫正理念都深受囚犯的认可。
Our prison guards are large and excellent, with a wealth of experience and expertise in law enforcement, and our management methods and correctional concepts are well recognized by prisoners.

我们的设施齐全，包括监舍、食堂、医务室、图书室、体育场等，为囚犯提供了良好的学习和生活条件。
Our facilities include prisons, canteens, infirmaries, libraries, stadiums, etc., which provide good learning and living conditions for prisoners.
